~Welcome to Honeybew~

Every great idea starts somewhere. Mine started with a group of friends, our favorite drinks, and way too many conversations over them. This website is a little collection of those drinks and the memories that came with them. Hopefully it leaves you smiling and maybe craving one too. I hope you have fun exploring this website.

This is the part where websites usually say something inspiring and professional. Instead, here's the truth, I made this with determination, Google, and a tiny bit of panic. Thanks for stopping by, your visit makes all the debugging feel worth it.

Before you go, here's a reminder to stay hydrated, try something new once in a while, and never underestimate the power of a good drink to brighten your day.
